South Florida's Middle School Debate Team

Since 2012, Future Leaders and Public Speakers has been working to bring the art of debate and speech to middle school and elementary school children in the greater Miami area through the facilitation of after school group lessons and the conduction of regional tournaments. FLPS strives to to teach children how to approach public speaking, develop their language skills, and think critically about current global issues by discussing high school and college level debate topics.
